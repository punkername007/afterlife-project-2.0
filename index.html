About

This project was launched by Tale of us (Italian Dj duo) on mid-June of 2016 at Barcelona’s outdoor Plaza Mayor de el Poble Espanyol. Through meticulous musical curation and a more experimental, concert-like approach, Afterlife sonically revived Ibiza. Characterised by mesmerising soundscapes and lush rhythms, the lineups included Recondite, Nina Kraviz, Dixon, Ame, Mind Against, Vaal, Mano Le Tough and many more djs.Afterlife label aims to take people on a journey, whether it comes to their events or releases.These two complement each other, with one aim; to bring the crowd into another dimension of perception with enthralling production that ignites the senses.An electronic music event concept characterized by mesmerizing soundscapes and lush rhythms designed for the senses. From hypnotic bass lines to energetic crescendos, the nuance of an immersive experience is there for the exploring.
